Who We Are

NaiSmart is a digital-first platform designed to empower matatu SACCOs in Nairobi through smart technology. We are a team of developers, transport specialists, and urban planners working together to bring transparency, efficiency, and growth to the heart of Kenya's public transport system.

Our mission is to revolutionize SACCO operations by offering digital tools that simplify fleet management, fare tracking, route planning, and investor reporting — while giving commuters real-time access and reliable service.
